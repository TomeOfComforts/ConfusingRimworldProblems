Welcome to North Carolina

From the windswept cliffs of Cape Hatteras to the majestic Blue Ridge and stunning Smoky Mountains, North Carolina blends rich history, cities empowered by the spiraling aspirations of great research, and diverse natural beauty.

Explore a few of the best cities with our scenic city series—Raleigh (the capital), Winston-Salem, and Greenville—using the navigation above. For official statewide services, visit nc.gov (opens in new tab).

Quick facts

  • Capital: Raleigh
  • Regions: Coastal Plain, Piedmont, Mountains
  • Nicknames: Old North State, Tar Heel State
­

Top 3 reasons people visit

  1. Beaches and maritime history along the Outer Banks
  2. Blue Ridge Parkway vistas and hiking
  3. Food, arts, and innovation across the Triangle and Triad

Industries and strengths

  • Research and technology (RTP: Raleigh–Durham–Chapel Hill)
  • Manufacturing, aviation, and logistics
  • Healthcare and higher education (ECU, UNC, NC State, Duke)
